🚀 Logo Setup Instructions

⚠️ Important: Replace the placeholder logo

To display your Smoking Snakes logo on the website, you need to:

📁 Step 1: Add Your Logo File

Place your Smoking Snakes logo image file in the public folder with the name logo.png

public/ ├── logo.png ← Add your logo here ├── index.html └── ...

🎨 Step 2: Logo Specifications

  • Format: PNG (preferred) or JPG
  • Background: Transparent or black background works best
  • Dimensions: Recommended 400x120px or similar aspect ratio
  • File size: Keep under 500KB for fast loading

🔧 Step 3: Logo Placement

Your logo will automatically appear in:

  • 📱 Header navigation (top of every page)
  • 🏠 Hero section on the homepage
  • ⬇️ Footer section (bottom of every page)

💡 Tips

  • Use a high-resolution version of your logo
  • Ensure good contrast against dark backgrounds
  • Test how it looks on mobile devices
  • Consider creating different sizes for different screen resolutions

🎯 Current Status

The website is currently using placeholder text. Once you add logo.png to the public folder, your logo will automatically appear!

🔄 After Adding the Logo

Once you add the logo file:

  1. Save the file as logo.png in the public folder
  2. The website will automatically refresh and display your logo
  3. If it doesn't refresh automatically, refresh your browser
